# 🏥 Health Care Analytics Dashboard

---

# 📌 1. Project Title

**Health Care Analytics Dashboard**

An interactive Power BI Business Intelligence Dashboard that analyzes patient demographics, lifestyle habits, medical conditions, and health risk indicators to provide meaningful healthcare insights. The dashboard enables healthcare professionals, researchers, and decision-makers to monitor patient health, identify high-risk individuals, and support data-driven clinical and preventive healthcare decisions through interactive visualizations.

---

# 📖 2. Short Description

The **Health Care Analytics Dashboard** transforms raw healthcare data into actionable insights by analyzing patient demographics, chronic diseases, lifestyle behaviors, and overall health status. It provides an intuitive interface to monitor disease prevalence, evaluate health risks, compare demographic trends, and support evidence-based healthcare planning.

---

# 🛠️ 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Interactive dashboard creation and reporting.
- 🔄 **Power Query** – Data cleaning, preprocessing, and transformation.
- 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Custom measures, calculated columns, and KPIs.
- 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Building relationships between healthcare data tables.
- 📈 **Power BI Visualizations** – KPI Cards, Pie Charts, Donut Charts, Bar Charts, Funnel Charts, Treemap, Area Chart, and Slicers.

### 📁 File Formats

# ✨ 5. Features

## 📈 Business Problem

Healthcare organizations collect extensive patient data, including demographic details, lifestyle habits, and medical history. Without an effective analytics platform, identifying health trends, monitoring chronic diseases, and prioritizing high-risk patients becomes challenging.

### Healthcare stakeholders need answers to questions such as:

- Which patients belong to high or critical health risk categories?
- Which age groups are most vulnerable to health risks?
- How common are heart attacks and strokes among patients?
- How do lifestyle habits such as smoking, exercise, and diet influence overall health?
- Which demographic groups have higher overweight prevalence?
- What is the overall health distribution across the patient population?

---

## 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard aims to:

- Monitor overall patient health status.
- Identify high-risk and critical-risk patients.
- Analyze heart attack and stroke prevalence.
- Evaluate health conditions across different age groups.
- Compare overweight prevalence among racial groups.
- Study the impact of lifestyle habits on health outcomes.
- Enable interactive healthcare analytics using dynamic filters.
